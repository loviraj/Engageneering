How Certificate Verification Works

1
Contribution Detected — When a user posts their first question or answer in a cluster, the system automatically evaluates their eligibility.
2
Certificate Issued — A unique Engageneer certificate is created, stored securely in our database with a tamper-proof ID.
3
Verification Link Generated — Each certificate gets a permanent public URL that anyone can use to verify its authenticity — no login needed.
4
Live Database Check — This page queries our live Supabase database in real time, ensuring certificates cannot be faked or backdated.
