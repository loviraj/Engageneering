ARIA & AI Policy
Effective: 1 May 2026  ·  Version: 1.0

1. What ARIA Is

ARIA (Autonomous Routing & Intelligence Arbiter) is Engageneering™'s proprietary AI assessment system. It evaluates user contributions using multiple specialised agents across five scoring dimensions: Explanation Intelligence (EI), Relevance Index (RI), Relevance-Adjusted Intelligence (RAI), Conceptual Originality Index (COI), and Articulation Index (AI).

2. Limitations of AI Assessment

⚠ AI Limitations Notice: ARIA is an automated system. Like all AI, it may produce assessments that are inaccurate, incomplete, biased, or inconsistent. ARIA scores reflect the system's algorithmic evaluation only and should not be relied upon as definitive judgments of knowledge, competence, or teaching ability.

3. Ask ARIA

The Ask ARIA feature uses the Anthropic Claude API to synthesise answers from platform content. These AI-generated responses:

  • Are generated by an AI model and may contain errors or hallucinations
  • Do not constitute professional advice of any kind
  • Are not reviewed by Engageneering™ staff before delivery
  • Should be treated as a starting point for inquiry, not a definitive answer

4. Data Used for AI Processing

Your questions and answers may be sent to Anthropic's API for processing as part of the Ask ARIA feature. By using Ask ARIA, you consent to this processing in accordance with Anthropic's privacy policy. We do not use your personal data to train AI models without your explicit consent.

5. Bias and Fairness

We are committed to ongoing monitoring of ARIA for systemic bias across language, topic, culture, and user demographics. If you believe ARIA has assessed your contribution unfairly due to language, cultural context, or topic area, please contact aria@engageneering.org.

6. Human Oversight

Engageneering™ reserves the right to manually review, adjust, or override any ARIA assessment in cases of apparent system error, user complaint, or legal necessity. Manual overrides are recorded and logged.
