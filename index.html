The Intelligence Engine

Meet ARIA

ARIA — Autonomous Routing & Intelligence Arbiter — is the world's first fully autonomous crowd learning orchestrator. Twelve specialist agents, zero human intervention, every decision auditable and explainable.

Every question screened. Every answer scored on five independent indices. Every tier calculated on evidence. Every insight personalised. All in real time, all transparent, all defensible at any level.

ARIA · v2 · 12 Agents
Intake agents — trigger on question submission
Agent 01
Gatekeeper
Screens every question for harmful or bad-faith content. Approve / revise / reject with full reason. Nothing passes without clearance.
Agent 02
Question Rater
Scores on clarity, depth, originality, and engagement potential. Quality Score 0–100. Low scores return with improvement prompts.
Agent 03
Smart Matcher
Reads the question. Scans expertise, answer history, and tags. Personalised invitations to the top 5 best-fit answerers only.
Atomic evaluation agents — six run in parallel, each produces one index
Agent 04
Engagement Indexer
EI · 25% weight
Narrative pull, hook strength, questions raised — does the answer make the reader lean in?
Agent 05
Retention Indexer
RI · 20% weight
Will this be remembered in 7 days? Anchors, analogy density, sticky quotability scored.
Agent 06
Applicability Indexer
RAI · 25% weight
Can this be used on Monday morning? Context specificity, step clarity, theory-to-practice bridge.
Agent 07
Creativity Indexer
COI · 15% weight
Fresh perspective or restatement? Novelty of framing, unexpected connections, metaphor originality.
Agent 08
Accuracy Verifier
AI · 15% weight
Factually correct and well-grounded? Claim verifiability, logical coherence, error detection.
Agent 09
Integrity Guard
IF · binary gate
Plagiarism, hallucination, bad faith. An IF suspends the answer and overrides all index scores.
Grade arbiter & growth agents — the Composite Defended Score
Agent 10
Comparative Grade Arbiter
Blends Algorithmic Grade (AG) with Engageneer™ Peer Grade (PG) into the Composite Defended Score. Raises divergence flag if scores differ by more than 15 points.
Agent 11
Tier Engine
Monitors cumulative CDS history and milestones. Triggers tier promotions and demotions with full audit trail available on request.
Agent 12
Insight Narrator
Personalised weekly reports — index breakdowns, growth edges, next-action recommendations — in the Engageneering™ voice.
The Composite Defended Score — published, stable, transparent
AG = (EI × 0.25) + (RI × 0.20) + (RAI × 0.25) + (COI × 0.15) + (AI × 0.15)
CDS = (AG × 0.60) + (PG × 0.40)
Integrity flag raised → CDS suspended  |  |AG − PG| > 15 → Divergence flag → mandatory second review
Every weight is published. Every index is explained in plain language. Every CDS can be broken into its five components on request. The same formula applies to every answer, every answerer, every tier level — no exceptions.
Algorithmic Grade (AG)
Engageneering™ AI Grade
Produced by Agents 04–08. Objective, instant, consistent. Cannot be influenced by bias or personal relationships. Challengeable only with evidence of a specific indexing error.
Peer Grade (PG)
Engageneer™ Human Grade
Produced by a certified Engageneer™. Mandatory written justification per criterion. Adds contextual wisdom that no algorithm can fully replicate. Subject to divergence review.
The Framework

Six Pillars of Engagement

Everything we build starts from one question — what does it actually take for a student to lean in?

Pillar 01
Active Learning Design
Every learner does something with knowledge — always. Transform any lesson into a participation-first experience.
Pillar 02
Curiosity Engineering
Design the question before you deliver the answer. Build the hunger before serving the meal.
Pillar 03
Peer Connection Systems
Engagement is fundamentally social. Structure the community — the learning will follow.
Pillar 04
Attention Architecture
Design with the brain, not against it. Cognitive science applied practically, not theoretically.
Pillar 05
Real-Time Feedback Loops
Feedback is information, not judgment. Continuous, low-stakes, high-frequency — always actionable.
Pillar 06
Impact Measurement
Measure what actually matters. Replace grades with rich evidence of real learning.
Crowd Learning Platform

Two Tracks. One Destination.

Every member climbs one of two ladders — Seeker or Answerer — each earning status through the quality of their contribution, not the quantity. Both converge at the rarest status of all.

Seeker Track
T1
Curious
Joins. Asks first questions. Absorbs community norms. Votes on answers.
T2
Questioner
Asks consistently. Questions are well-formed, rated high by community.
T3
Challenger
Pushes answers further. Reveals gaps. Separates surface from deep.
T4
Pro Challenger
Questions shape the direction of knowledge. Proposes new categories.
T5
Knowledge Architect
Designs the learning landscape. Structures pathways. Bridges both tracks.
Answerer Track
T1
Responder
Joins. Begins answering. Learning what engaging answers feel like.
T2
Explainer
Answers show clarity and structure. Goes beyond facts to meaning.
T3
Illuminator
Answers shift how the asker sees the question. Wins Best Answer regularly.
T4
Teacher
Teaches frameworks. Creates lasting resources. Multiple seekers return.
T5
Master Teacher
Canonical reference in their domain. Trains Teachers. Shapes the canon.
